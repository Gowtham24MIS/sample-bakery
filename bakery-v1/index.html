Our Story

Baked with Soul,
Shared with Love

La Douceur began in a tiny kitchen in 2014, when chef Priya Menon started baking sourdough loaves for her neighbors. What started as a passion became a purpose — and soon, a beloved neighborhood institution.

Today, we craft over 500 artisan baked goods daily, using heritage grain flours, single-origin chocolate, and farm-fresh dairy — because you deserve nothing less than extraordinary.
